Unifeet Smart Running Insoles
User's Guide

Welcome to the Unifeet Smart Insoles User's Guide

1.Charging

Fully charge the battery when first use.

  • Charging -- Indicator light is on.
  • Check battery remains through the App.

2.Activate the Smart Insoles

Step for 3 seconds to activate the Smart Insoles.

  • Remove other insoles before use.

3.Install the App（UNI-FIT）

Please scan the QR code below to install the UNI-FIT app.

4.Bind with Your Phone

Please turn on the Bluetooth before you start the UNI-FIT app, follow the instructions to finish register and login.

Click "Devices" and follow the prompted pages to complete the device binding.

  • If binding with the App failed, please turn off the App and Bluetooth, and then restart.
  • If you only connect one Smart Insole, there might be data daviation.

Safety Instructions

1.Please charge with the original USB Charge Cable.

2.The output voltage of adapter must be 5V DC, output current ≥ 500mA. Please do not exceed this voltage value.

3.Please do not disassemble, tailor, fold or refit the product and its accessories yourself.

4.Please do not soak the insoles in liquid (e.g. water, alcohol, oil, etc.)

5.This product was built-in battery, please avoid fire and impact.

6.Please observe the disposal regulations for electronics and accessories and kindly support recycling.

Please see FAQ.

Care Instructions

Please use cloth or wet soft brush to clean the surface when it was dirty.

Please let it dry naturally if it is wet, do not use the hair dryer or others to dry it.
